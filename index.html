BIOGRAPHY

Early Life and Formation

LANY (pronounced "Lay-nee") was formed in 2014 by Paul Jason Klein, Jake Goss, and Les Priest. The band started in Nashville and quickly gained attention by uploading songs like "Hot Lights" and "Walk Away" online.

Music Style and Growth

LANY is known for their dreamy pop sound, combining electronic elements with emotional lyrics. Their music often talks about love, heartbreak, and youth experiences, making them popular among young listeners worldwide.

Recent Activities

LANY continues to release albums and perform globally. Their tours attract thousands of fans, and they remain one of the most recognized indie pop bands of their generation.
